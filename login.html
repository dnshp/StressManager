Welcome to your personal stress analyzer.
Being a student means taking care of your mental health.
A student's life can get hectic and messy. Stress Manager allows you to write how you feel after events or at the end of the day. If it detects that you're stressed,
it'll suggest activities for you to do to destress based on what you've logged before. Use it to keep in touch with your wellbeing during your school career.
